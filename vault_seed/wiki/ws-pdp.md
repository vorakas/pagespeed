# Workstream: Product Detail Page (PDP)

## Description

This workstream covers the Product Detail Page (PDP) and its associated load rules. It is the largest workstream by task count, encompassing product display customizations, stock indicators, open box variants, bundles, deep linking, ceiling fan configurations, recommendations, and product attributes. The load rules epic governs how product data is fetched and rendered.

## Scope

- **[[ACE2E-27 - AC E2E - PDP|ACE2E-27]]: PDP** (Evaluated) — All PDP layout, features, and customizations.
- **[[ACE2E-50 - AC E2E - Load Rules for PDP & PLA Product Panel|ACE2E-50]]: Load Rules for PDP & PLA** (Evaluated) — Product load rules governing data retrieval and display logic.
