# Concentrix Catalyst (Implementation Vendor)

Concentrix Catalyst is the primary implementation vendor for the Adobe Commerce migration. They provide design, SEO, analytics, and engineering services.

## Key People

| Name | Role | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| Megan Anaya | Project Owner | Owner of both LAMPSPLUS and LPWE Asana projects |

## Scope of Work

### LAMPSPLUS (Migration)
Full Adobe Commerce Cloud migration including:
- Comprehensive project discovery
- Digital design, SEO, analytics, and engineering
- Integration with OMS, PIM, and internal eCommerce systems
- Adobe Commerce Cloud hosting
- Waterfall/agile hybrid methodology

### LPWE (Managed Services)
Post-migration T&M support including:
- New features and enhancements
- Bug fixes
- Security patches and upgrades
- Project management and QA support
