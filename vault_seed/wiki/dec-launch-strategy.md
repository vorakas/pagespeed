# Decision: Soft Launch Strategy

**Date:** Various (2025)
**Status:** Accepted

## Strategy
Phased rollout approach for the Adobe Commerce migration:
1. **Kiosks** — In-store kiosk hardware first
2. **CSRs** — Customer service representatives
3. **10% customers** — Limited public traffic
4. **Increased %** — Gradual ramp-up
5. **Full Launch** — 100% traffic on Adobe Commerce

## Current Target
- Soft launch: April 22-29, 2026
- Original target: July/August 2025
